🍛 Jollof Rice

Details

  • Prep Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 40 minutes
  • Servings: 4 people

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Rice 2 cups
Tomato paste 3 spoons
Onions 1
Spices To taste

Steps

  1. Wash the rice thoroughly.
  2. Fry onions and tomato paste in oil.
  3. Add water and spices.
  4. Add rice and cook until soft.

🥞 Pancakes

Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Servings: 2 people

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1
Sugar 2 spoons

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients into a smooth batter.
  2. Heat a pan and pour batter.
  3. Flip when bubbles appear.
  4. Serve hot with syrup or fruit.
