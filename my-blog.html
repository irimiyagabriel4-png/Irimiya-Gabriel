My First Step Into Web Development
June 2026

Starting my journey into programming has been both exciting and challenging. I began by learning the basics of HTML and CSS, which helped me understand how websites are structured and styled.

At first, it felt overwhelming, but as I continued practicing, I started to see how everything connects. Creating simple pages gave me confidence and motivated me to keep learning more every day.

Building My First Projects
June 2026

After learning the basics, I started building real projects like a travel website and a recipe book. These projects helped me understand how to organize content, use CSS styling, and create user-friendly layouts.

I also learned the importance of clean design and structure, which is very similar to UX/UI design principles. Each project I complete makes me more confident and brings me closer to becoming a professional developer.
