Product Analysis: WhatsApp

1. App Name

WhatsApp

UX Good Point

One strong UX feature of WhatsApp is its simple and fast messaging process. Users can send messages, images, and voice notes quickly with very few steps, making the app easy to use even for beginners.

3. UI Good Point

WhatsApp has a clean and simple interface with clear and readable fonts. The chat layout is well organized, making it easy for users to navigate and read messages without confusion.

4. Area to Improve

One area for improvement is the Status feature. It can be confusing for some users because it is separated from chats and not always easy to understand or access.
