Persona and User Journey

1. User Persona

Name: Amina Ibrahim
Age: 28
Location: Maiduguri, Nigeria
Occupation: Teacher
Goal: To easily book a doctor's appointment online without stress.
Behavior: Uses a smartphone daily and prefers simple, fast apps.
Challenge: Gets confused when websites are slow or complicated.

2. User Journey Steps

  1. Amina opens her phone and visits a hospital website.
  2. She searches for available doctors and appointment options.
  3. She selects a doctor and chooses a date and time.
  4. She fills in her personal details and confirms the booking.
  5. She receives a confirmation message.

3. Pain Point

A major pain point is that the website may be slow and require too many steps to book an appointment, which can confuse or frustrate the user.
