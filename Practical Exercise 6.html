2. Accessibility Evaluation

Contrast Issue Found:

Initially, the button used a lighter blue color, which made the white text difficult to read. The contrast ratio was too low for accessibility standards.

Adjustment Made:

  • Changed the button color to a darker blue (#0066cc)
  • Ensured white text has strong contrast against the button
  • Improved readability for users with visual impairments

After adjustment, the text became clear and accessible, meeting basic contrast requirements.
